# Draw Inspiration — Product Overview

## What is it?

Draw Inspiration is a creative prompt generator for artists and illustrators.

Instead of staring at a blank page, you open the app, tap once, and receive a rich, cinematic drawing prompt — not just a subject, but an entire atmosphere. A scene with mood, lighting, character, and emotional weight.

> *"A night-shift lighthouse keeper who catalogues ships that never arrive, standing at a fogged window while a candle burns low."*

---

## Who is it for?

Anyone who draws, sketches, paints, or just wants to. Especially useful for:

- Artists who want a daily creative prompt without the blandness of generic generators
- Illustrators building a body of work with a consistent aesthetic
- Students in sketchbook practice who need starting points
- Creative people who think visually and want prompts that feel intentional

---

## The problem it solves

Generic prompt generators say things like "draw a cat" or "draw a forest at night." They're fine, but they don't spark anything. They leave all the creative decisions — mood, atmosphere, tension, character — back to you.

Draw Inspiration does the opposite. Each prompt is a scene, not a subject. It gives you:

- **A title** — evocative, like a short film
- **The full scene** — character, environment, action, atmosphere in 2–3 sentences
- **A breakdown** — subject, environment, mood, lighting, twist, constraint spelled out
- **Aesthetic chips** — visual vocabulary tags like `DUSK`, `FLEETING`, `WARMTH`

You still make all the artistic decisions. But the imaginative starting point is already rich.

---

## The core idea

Three forces combine to make each prompt:

1. **AI generation** — a language model (Groq's Llama 3.3) trained to write evocative creative scenarios
2. **Style boards** — 56 hand-curated reference boards organized by mood (light, figure, time, memory, tension, quiet, myth) that inject visual vocabulary
3. **User composition** — you can guide the generator by setting mood, subject, style, constraint, and twist before generating

Used together, they produce prompts that feel specific and authored — not random.

---

## Core values

**Prompt quality over quantity.** One deeply felt prompt beats ten generic ones.

**No account, no friction.** You open it, you use it. Nothing to sign up for.

**Your library is yours.** Everything saves to your device. Export it anytime as JSON.

**Works in Portuguese too.** Full Brazilian Portuguese support — and the AI generates in the selected language.

## What it's not

- Not a social platform. No accounts, no feeds, no following.
- Not a writing tool. It gives you drawing prompts, not stories.
- Not a subscription. No payment, no tiers, no paywalls.
